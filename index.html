Privacy Policy for SimplePad

Effective Date: January 2025

SimplePad is an offline note-taking application developed by Windo Flash.

1. Information Collection

SimplePad does not collect, store, or share any personal information. All notes are stored locally on your device.

2. Internet Usage

This app does not transmit any user data over the internet.

3. Permissions

SimplePad does not request sensitive permissions such as location, contacts, camera, or microphone access.

4. Third-Party Services

This app does not use advertising networks, analytics tools, or tracking services.

5. Data Security

All notes remain on your device. The developer does not have access to your data.

6. Data Deletion

All app data is removed when the app is uninstalled.
